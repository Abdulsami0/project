Pet Care Tips

Keep your furry and feathered friends healthy and happy!

Dog Care
  • ✔️ Walk daily for physical and mental health
  • ✔️ Regular vet checkups and vaccinations
  • ✔️ Feed nutritious dog food and fresh water
  • ✔️ Groom and bathe once a month
Cat Care
  • ✔️ Provide scratching posts and toys
  • ✔️ Clean litter box regularly
  • ✔️ Balanced diet with clean water
  • ✔️ Keep indoors for safety
Bird Care
  • ✔️ Clean cage and fresh food daily
  • ✔️ Provide toys for stimulation
  • ✔️ Regular vet checks for feathers and beak
  • ✔️ Allow safe flight time indoors
